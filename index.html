Welcome to Chennai Museum

Chennai Museum

Discover the Chennai Museum, a treasure trove of art and culture. Established through the vision of the Madras Literary Society in 1846, the museum opened its doors in 1851 under the guidance of Dr. Edward Balfour. Initially located in the College of Fort St. George, the museum evolved over the years, moving to the Pantheon in 1854, where it thrived as a cultural hub. The museum's rich history reflects the dedication of its officers, who have tirelessly curated collections that preserve the heritage of our community. From its beginnings to the present day, the Chennai Museum has played a pivotal role in showcasing the region's diverse cultural legacy.
